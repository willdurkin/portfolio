About Us

Rex Collective is a team of NYC women driven by curiosity and the need to take risks. The Rex Collective girl challenges the conventional positioning of male and female in opposition, antithesis or conflict. Our designs tell a story about what it means to be at once vulnerable and strong, sexual but not sexualized, loving and independent.
